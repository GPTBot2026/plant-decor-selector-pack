# Sample Output

## Space Summary
- Small bedroom corner near a north-facing window
- Medium to low light
- One cat in the home
- Wants a calm, minimal look
- Budget: USD 40 to 70
- Prefers low maintenance

## Best-Fit Recommendation
Choose a cast iron plant in a simple matte ceramic pot in off-white or sand. It fits the low-maintenance requirement, tolerates lower light better than many trendy options, and supports a calm minimal look.

## Plant Directions

### Option 1: Cast Iron Plant
- Why it fits: strong tolerance for inconsistent care and lower light
- Look: upright, clean, understated
- Risk notes: slow grower, but stable for beginners

### Option 2: Parlor Palm
- Why it fits: soft look, beginner-friendly, common indoors
- Look: lighter and more relaxed than rigid architectural plants
- Risk notes: prefers some humidity consistency

### Option 3: Peperomia Obtusifolia
- Why it fits: compact, forgiving, good for shelf or small stand styling
- Look: tidy, glossy, neat
- Risk notes: do not overwater

## Pot Directions
- matte ceramic cylinder planter
- small plant stand with neutral pot
- textured stone-look pot for a softer decor accent

## Shopping Checklist
- confirm pet safety before purchase
- check pot drainage or add a nursery pot insert
- avoid oversized planters for first setup
- place within realistic light, not idealized light
