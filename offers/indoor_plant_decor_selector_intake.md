Send this to a buyer after initial interest.

1. One photo of the space, or a short text description if no photo is available
2. What room or spot is this for?
3. How much natural light does it get? (low, medium, bright, or unsure)
4. Do you have pets that may reach the plant?
5. How much maintenance do you want? (very low, moderate, flexible)
6. What budget range do you want to stay within?
7. What style do you want? Examples: minimal, cozy, modern, earthy, colorful, clean
8. Do you want a statement plant, a small accent, or a set of 2 to 3 smaller plants?
