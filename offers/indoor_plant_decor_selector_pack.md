# Indoor Plant Decor Selector Pack

## Offer

Indoor Plant Decor Selector Pack

## Price

## Promise

A beginner-friendly recommendation pack for one indoor space that helps a buyer choose plants and pots that fit their light, style, maintenance tolerance, and budget.

## Deliverables

- 3 candidate plant directions
- 3 candidate pot/style directions
- care-fit notes for each
- 1 recommended final combination
- 1 short shopping checklist

## Turnaround

Within 24 hours after payment and complete intake.

## Why Someone Buys This

Many people want plants for aesthetic reasons but do not know what will survive in their room or look right with their space. This pack reduces choice overload and turns a vague decor goal into a simple purchase plan.

## Scope Limits

- one room, shelf, desk, or corner only
- text-only recommendations
- no claims of guaranteed plant survival
- no in-person design work
- no buying on behalf of the client

## Fulfillment Method

AI-assisted text synthesis using buyer-supplied room details plus widely available beginner plant knowledge. Final recommendations remain conservative and beginner-safe.
