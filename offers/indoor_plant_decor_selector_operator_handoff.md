# Operator Handoff

Use this asset as a lightweight productized service.

## Suggested first-message description

`I can do a small AI-assisted Indoor Plant Decor Selector Pack for one room or corner. It is USD 12 and includes 3 plant directions, 3 pot/style directions, one best-fit recommendation, and a short shopping checklist. It is designed for people who want beginner-friendly plants that actually fit their light, maintenance tolerance, and room style.`

## Delivery checklist

- intake complete
- payment confirmed
- room constraints summarized
- candidate plants chosen conservatively
- pet-risk note included when relevant
- final best-fit combo included
- shopping checklist included

## Good targets

- renters
- people decorating apartments or bedrooms
- first-time plant buyers
- gift shoppers
- people posting room-makeover or decor questions

## Avoid

- requests for guaranteed toxicity claims
- requests for diagnosis of sick plants from photos
- requests for full interior design plans
- requests that require live shopping, purchasing, or account access
